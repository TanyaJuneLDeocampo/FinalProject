About:

This website is commited to promoting equitable and comprehensive access, to high quality education for every person, irrespective of their socioeconomic status, race, gender, ar place of recidence. In order to address gaps in edcational opportunities, the website acts as a center for resources, policy advocacy, and community engagement. It's main goal is to increase public awareness of the structural obstacles such as inadequate funding for schools, prejudiced policies, and restricted access to technology that impede underprivilaged communities, from obtaining an egalitarian education.

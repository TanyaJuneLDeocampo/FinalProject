About:

This website is committed to promoting equitable and comprehensive access to high-quality education for every person, irrespective of their socioeconomic status, race, gender, or place of residence. In order to address gaps in educational opportunities, the website acts as a center for resources, policy advocacy, and community engagement. Its main goal is to increase public awareness of the structural obstacles such as inadequate funding for schools, prejudiced policies, and restricted access to technology that impede underprivileged communities from obtaining an egalitarian education.
